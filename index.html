Get ready for thrill

11/7/2021

First five seasons of American horror story is now available on our streaming service for registered users. Series will be available on demand. Availability of another 4 seasons is being negotiated and should be available in early 2022. TastyTV will keep you updated on this topic as negotiations with creators Brad Falchuk and Ryan Murphy are going to happen over autumn 2021.

AHS is an anthology series centering on different characters and locations, including a house with a murderous past, an insane asylum, a witch coven, a freak show circus, a haunted hotel, a possessed farmhouse, a cult, the apocalypse, and a slasher summer camp.

After long conversations with HBO, all seasons of Game of Thrones are now available on our on demand service. Customers can start watching on Friday 25/6/2021.

Game of Thrones is action / adventure story of nine noble families who fight for control over the lands of Westeros, while an ancient enemy returns after being dormant for millennia.
